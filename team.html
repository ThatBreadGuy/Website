Our team

Meet the team that works for TheSharks.

Owners

Dougley

Nationality: Dutch
Position: Main developer, Owner

Dougley is the main developer of WildBeast and leads TheSharks. He is the shot caller in the team and takes care of server things. He's an experienced developer and a decently big name in the Discord development community.

Mirrow

Nationality: Dutch
Position: Co-owner, Secondary developer

Mirrow is the second owner of TheSharks and figures around the server doing a bit of this and that. Second WildBeast version 1.0.0 developer along with Dougley and Perpetucake. Also regarded as a royal Snek.

SuperStaff

Zaza

Nationality: Italian
Position: Developer, SuperStaff, Administrator, Database engineer

Zaza is head of development. He can act as a backup in case Doug is absent and server actions need to be performed. Makes regular code contributions and assists in server maintenance. Upholds a mirroring database for WildBot.

Curtis

Nationality: Australian
Position: SuperStaff, Administrator

Curtis is a SuperStaff member and occasional code contributor. He is known for developing his own fork of WildBeast, called Marshmallow. Online when most others sleep due to being from the land down under. But don't mention him or you're going to have a bad time.

LWTechGaming

Nationality: Finnish
Position: SuperStaff, Administrator, Translator, Website developer, Head documentation writer

LW is the newest addition to WildBeast SuperStaff. He has made the Finnish and Swedish translations for WildBeast. Perhaps best known for writing and maintaining the documentation for WildBeast. In addition to this he does web development with Dougley. Jack of all trades.

Admins

Nightex

Nationality: German

Nenkai

Nenkai, formerly known as TheKill69, is the most active contributor to WildBeast GitHub repository rivaled only by Dougley. He provides mainly bugfixes and runs his own WildBeast fork, Sinister. Also the author of the French translation for WB.

Dean

Kayo
